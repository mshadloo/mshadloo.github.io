    • Biography

      I am a Ph.D. candidate in the EECS Department , at UC Merced; I'm working under supervision of Prof. Sungjin Im. I study the design and analysis of approximation and online algorithms. The algorithms that guarantee to return solution as close as optimal solution in efficient time. My research focuses on addressing algorithmic challenges arising in large large data-centers and cloud computing environments.
      Prior to Ph.D. I got my M.Sc in Artificial Intelligence in Computer Engineering department of Sharif University of Technology. In my M.Sc studies I worked on machine learning algorithms. My research focused on active learning for spam filtering problem.
      I received my B.Sc in Software Engineering in Computer Engineering department of Amirkabir University of Technology.
